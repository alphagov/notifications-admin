In trial mode there are limits on:

  • who you can send messages to
  • how many messages you can send each day

To remove these restrictions, you’ll need to make your service live.

{% set how_to_make_service_live_html %}

When you have finished testing your service:

  1. Go to the Make your service live page.
  2. Complete the tasks.
  3. Select Send a request to go live.

It can take up to one working day to make your service live.

This is so we can check that you’ve met our terms of use (opens in new tab).
