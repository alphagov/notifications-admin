How it works

Once you verify your sender name, Notify will use RCS to send your messages.

RCS (Rich Communication Services) is a newer, more secure type of text messaging.

{% set who_can %}

Currently, about 20% of phones can receive RCS messages. By the end of 2024 we expect the number to be more than 50%.

If a recipient’s phone is not compatible with RCS, Notify will send a standard text message instead.

You cannot send RCS messages to international phone numbers.
