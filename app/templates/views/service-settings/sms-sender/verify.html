Before you verify your sender ID

You will need to:

  • provide contact details for your service
  • upload a logo

It takes around 2 weeks to get your sender ID verified.

Pricing

Verified messages cost the same as or less than normal text messages.

There is no monthly charge or setup fee.

Who can receive verified messages

  • about 20% of phones can receive verified text messages now
  • by the end of 2024 we expect the number to be more than 50%
  • verified text messages can’t be sent to international phone numbers
  • if a phone can’t receive verified text messages Notify will send a normal text message automatically
