If you have not received your security code

Wait a few minutes. Text messages sometimes take a while to arrive.

If this does not work:

  1. Check that you can receive text messages from other numbers.
  2. Switch your phone off and then back on to try and reset your mobile phone connection.
  3. If you still do not receive a security code, we can send you a new one.

{{ govukButton({ "element": "a", "text": "Resend security code", "href": url_for('main.check_and_resend_verification_code', next=redirect_url) }) }}

If this does not work, contact us.

If your phone number has changed

Find a member of your team with the ‘Manage settings, team and usage’ permission. Ask them to:

  1. Sign in to GOV.UK Notify.
  2. Go to the Team members page.
  3. Find your name and select Change details.
  4. Select Change next to your phone number.
  5. Enter your new phone number and select Save.
  6. Select Save again.

Now try to sign in again.

If you want to sign in with an email link instead

Ask a member of your team with the ‘Manage settings, team and usage’ permission to change your sign-in method.
