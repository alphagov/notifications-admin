Team members and permissions

Notify lets you set different permissions for each member of your team.

Go to the Team members page to:

  • invite people to your service
  • control who can see, add, edit and send messages
  • let other team members change your service settings
  • amend team members’ sign-in details
  • remove team members who have left

You must add at least one team member with the ‘manage settings, team and usage’ permission. This person will be responsible for your service if you’re unable to sign in to Notify.

You can invite team members who do not have a government email address.
