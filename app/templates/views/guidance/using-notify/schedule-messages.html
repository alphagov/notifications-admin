Schedule emails and text messages

You can schedule messages up to one week in advance.

To schedule a batch of emails or text messages:

  1. Go to the {{ service_link(current_service, 'main.choose_template', 'templates') }} page.
  2. Add a new template or choose an existing template.
  3. Select Get ready to send.
  4. Upload a list of recipients.
  5. Set the date and time you want to send your messages.

Your scheduled messages will appear on the {{ service_link(current_service, 'main.service_dashboard', 'dashboard') }} and {{ service_link(current_service, 'main.uploads', 'uploads') }} pages.
