GOV.UK Notify is available to teams from:

  • central government departments
  • local authorities
  • the armed forces
  • the NHS
  • the emergency services
  • GP surgeries
  • state-funded schools

If you work for one of these organisations but get an error when you try to create a profile, contact support.

GOV.UK Notify is not for personal use.

GOV.UK Notify is not available to:

  • charities
  • hospices
  • housing associations
  • members of the public
  • universities

Suppliers

If you’re doing work for a public sector organisation you can use GOV.UK Notify.

Someone from the public sector organisation you’re working with needs to create a profile then add a service. Then they can invite you to be a team member of that service.

GP surgeries

NHS-funded GP surgeries can use GOV.UK Notify, but they:

  • do not get an annual allowance of free text messages
  • cannot send any text messages in trial mode

This is because GP surgeries will be able to use NHS Notify to send messages.

NHS Notify should be available to GP surgeries in 2025.

Members of the public

The GOV.UK Notify service is only for people who work in the government or other public sector organisations.

Find government services and information on GOV.UK.
